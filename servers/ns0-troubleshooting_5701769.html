General troubleshooting hints

DHCP says no subnet declaration defined, this likely means that it is not pointing to /etc/dhcpd.conf but to /etc/dhcp/dhcpd.conf (which is empty)

If all slaves and master have the same DNS SOA record after making zone file changes, it means it is propagating healthily.

ntpstat will show you the NTP daemon status.. it should say "synchronised", "stratum 2", "time correct to within ~30ms"
